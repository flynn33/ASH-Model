# R-015 falsification addendum

R-015 introduces three immutable prospective/held-out ASH prediction entries in
`predictions/locked/r015_prediction_ledger.locked.json`.

## General rule

A locked prediction fails only under the preregistered held-out likelihood or compression declared for that prediction. Numerical claims, domains, nuisance policies, and falsification rules are frozen as of the ledger `freeze_date`.

A failed or revised prediction must be superseded by a new prediction ID. Do not mutate the R-015 ledger entries in place.

## Boundary

This addendum records falsification posture only. It does not apply external observational data, compute an empirical likelihood, or claim that any ASH prediction has been observed.
