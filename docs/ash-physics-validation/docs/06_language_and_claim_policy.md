# Language and Claim Policy

## Required claim boundary

The repository may state:

```text
ASH has a verified finite algebraic kernel and executable reference implementation for the stated finite objects.
```

Statements of completed physical confirmation are not allowed unless the corresponding validation gates have passed. Do not equate finite algebra verification, bell-shaped histograms, or Garden-algebra identities with confirmation of cosmology or physical supersymmetry.

## Safe wording

Use:

```text
proposed physical bridge
candidate dynamics
derived under stated assumptions
finite theorem
computational certificate
empirical validation pending
not yet observationally confirmed
requires held-out validation
```

Avoid:

```text
confirmed
proven physical theory
completed cosmology validation
demonstrates the universe
establishes reality
physical supersymmetry as an observed fact
measurement statistics confirmed
```

## Attribution policy

Do not include external authorship notices, vendor names, external process notes, or external-author boilerplate.

## Version policy

Any change to postulates, dynamics, bridge maps, likelihood choices, priors, data cuts, or prediction statistics creates a new validation version.

## Documentation policy

Every strong statement must identify its status:

- theorem;
- implementation result;
- numerical experiment;
- empirical result;
- hypothesis;
- interpretation.
