# R-010 Addendum — Unit-Bearing Observable Proxy Specification

R-010 adds named synthetic unit-bearing proxy observables produced from branch-ensemble features:

```text
time_s
coarse_length_m
scale_factor_dimensionless
H_bridge_s_inv
energy_density_J_m3
mass_density_kg_m3
einstein_curvature_proxy_m_inv2
memory_length_m
temperature_proxy_K
```

These columns are generated by `ash_model.unit_bridge.physical_bridge`.

The default calibration is fiducial and synthetic. The observable proxies may support future calibration and validation programs, but they are not telescope, particle, gravitational, or laboratory observables until reviewed external calibration, data products, covariance sources, matched baselines, and locked predictions are committed.
