# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and constructive environment for everyone participating in the ASH Model project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints, disciplines, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on reproducibility, clarity, and what is best for the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Racist, hateful, vulgar, obscene, or profane language
- Threats, intimidation, or encouraging self-harm
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or academic setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The project maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions and to ban contributors for behavior deemed inappropriate. Project discussions may also be moderated automatically against these standards.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
